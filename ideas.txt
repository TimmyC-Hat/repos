Level 0: The lobby
Before you is an expansive non-Euclidean space resembling 
the back rooms of a dated retail outlet. The constant 
hum-buzz emitted by the ceiling lights permeates every 
area of the Lobby. Above you is a small space, around 
one meter in height above the square boxes that make up
the ceiling. Scattered around the cramped ceiling space 
are wires, electrical fixtures, and ventilation ducts, 
making it unnavigatable. What do you do in this situation.
